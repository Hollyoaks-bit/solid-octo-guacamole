ALL ABOUT SALZBURG

Salzburg is a beautiful city in Austria, known for its rich history, elegant architecture, and breathtaking mountain scenery. The city is famously recognised as the birthplace of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, whose musical legacy continues to shape its cultural identity. Visitors enjoy exploring the narrow streets of the Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with charming shops, cafés, and historic buildings. The River Salzach flows peacefully through the city, offering stunning views of the surrounding hills. With its blend of music, culture, and natural beauty, Salzburg remains one of Europe’s most captivating destinations.
